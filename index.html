About Us

ElectroVision is a dynamic and innovative home electronics company based in Semarang, Indonesia. Founded with a vision to revolutionize the home electronics industry, we are dedicated to providing top-notch products that cater to the evolving needs of modern households. Our product range includes state-of-the-art Smart TVs with the latest display technologies, smart kitchen appliances designed to make cooking effortless and enjoyable, advanced IoT-based security systems that ensure the safety of your home, high-fidelity audio devices for an immersive listening experience, and energy-efficient air conditioners that keep your home comfortable while reducing energy consumption. At ElectroVision, we pride ourselves on our ability to combine cutting-edge technology with user-friendly designs, ensuring that our products not only meet but exceed our customers' expectations.

Our team is composed of highly skilled professionals with extensive experience in technology and customer service. We believe that the foundation of our success lies in our commitment to innovation, quality, and customer satisfaction. Our engineers and designers work tirelessly to stay ahead of technological trends, ensuring that every product we release is at the forefront of the industry. Additionally, our customer service team is dedicated to providing exceptional support, helping customers get the most out of their ElectroVision products. By maintaining a strong focus on these core values, we aim to build long-lasting relationships with our customers and establish ElectroVision as a trusted name in home electronics.

Vision & Mission

Vision

To become a leader in the home electronics industry by providing innovative and high-quality products that enhance the quality of life for our customers.

Mission

  1. Innovation: Develop innovative electronic products that keep pace with the latest technological advancements.
  2. Quality: Ensure every product we offer meets the highest quality standards.
  3. Customer Satisfaction: Deliver exceptional customer service by focusing on the needs and expectations of our customers.
  4. Sustainability: Implement environmentally friendly and sustainable business practices.
  5. Community: Make a positive contribution to society through various social and educational programs.
